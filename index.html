At the heart of your University of Delaware experience is Student Life. Led by the Vice President for Student Life, we’re here to support and empower you—our students—to grow personally, succeed academically, and thrive in your future career and life.

We offer many ways for you to discover your passions, prioritize well-being, and connect with others. From exploring your identity to preparing for lifelong success, Student Life is your trusted partner throughout your college journey and beyond.

The Division of Student Life promotes equity and inclusion, supports holistic development, and enhances student learning through diverse programs, transformative experiences, and vibrant communities that prepare you to succeed in all areas of your life.

We act with integrity, treat others with respect, and uplift all students and colleagues. When we fall short of our values, we hold ourselves and each other accountable—owning mistakes, listening openly, and working together to create a stronger, braver community.
